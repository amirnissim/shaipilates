Hi!

My name is Shai Zehavi and I'm a Pilates instructor, certified by PSC - Pilates Sport Center. I came across Pilates through dancing and spent the past years learning and evolving through the method in various classes both in Tel-Aviv and New York. I teach private classes and small groups in order to give full attention to each body and syndrome.

The Pilates method focuses on strengthening the deep muscles and improving flexibility and posture. It includes a large range of exercises and routines, therefore the Pilates is suitable for all body types and pathologies.

The sessions take place at the privacy of your own home, given that Pilates does not require more than basic tools which I provide. The first session includes diagnoses of physical status and building a plan for the client's personal needs.
